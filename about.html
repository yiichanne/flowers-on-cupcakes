Our Origins

Like many other bakers, baking started out as a hobby for us in our younger days. When Instagram came out, we had a great time looking through all the pretty cupcakes on Instagram and we started to share images of our creations. Soon, our friends and acquaintances commission us to bake for their parties and it slowly grew from there. We took a leap and created a shop which brings us to here now!

Our Goals

Our customers' satisfaction is our commitment. We aim to provide great customer service always and to make sure your party's cupcakes will be gorgeous for your social media!

Our Quality

We promise to only use the freshest ingredients and only deliver cupcakes that are freshly made. Hygiene standards are strictly adhered in our baking studio.
